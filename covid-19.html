COVID-19 Event Information

﻿​

   After much thought, consideration and prayer, Christian and I have come to the decision to postpone our May 17th Wedding to August 16th. The health and well being of our guests is a priority for us, and given current events, we feel it would be unwise to continue with the wedding as planned. We will be sending out a card with more information and directions on how to RSPV for the new date. We hope you can still make it!

   Although Christian and I are disappointed by this turn of events, we still feel incredibly fortunate and thankful for the people surrounding us. Christian and I are still getting married in May in a small ceremony with our closest family (don’t worry; we will practice good social distancing). The way I see it, Christian and I get to have our cake and eat it too! We get to have a small, intimate ceremony with the people we love most and a bigger ceremony and reception with the rest of the people we love and value. And besides, all of our friends, family and us are safe and healthy, our parents have kept their jobs, we are able to make rent, Christian still starts at his job in Atlanta in June, and we haven’t run out of toilet paper. There are worse things in life than having to postpone a party! We consider ourselves truly blessed.

If you would like to still send us wedding gifts in May instead of waiting until August, we would be extremely grateful. Our full wedding registry is available on our website. Thank you so much!

﻿​

   We love you all. Continue to stay safe and healthy, and let us know if you have any questions.
